# Instagram

The gallery. Our most polished room; crest art lives here best.

## Formats
- **Feed image:** 1080×1350 (4:5) preferred, 1080×1080 ok
- **Carousel:** up to 10 × 1080×1350 (crest lore, drops, explainers)
- **Reels:** 1080×1920 (9:16), <90s, the pixel-motion home
- **Stories:** 1080×1920, countdowns + matchday reminders

## Rules
- caption: lead with the hook in the first line (feed truncates ~125 chars)
- hashtags: 3-5 max, in a trailing block, never stuffed
- the pixel art does the talking; minimal emoji

## Posting (later, via aggregator or Graph API)
- requires Business/Creator account linked to a Facebook Page
- Graph API needs Meta app review for content publishing
