# Battle of the Pixels

Our signature ritual. After the final whistle, the loser's crest visibly
dithers/dissolves based on the scoreline. The worse the loss, the more it
breaks apart. Winner's crest stays crisp, tinted in its accent.

## Visual
- Animated (TikTok/Reels/Shorts): the 11-effect motion system drives the
  dissolve. Goal differential → % of pixels intact. xG ratio → saturation.
- Static fallback (X/IG feed): final frame of the dissolve.

## Caption formula
`[winner] [score] [loser].`
`[loser]'s crest is still buffering. 📉`

## Example
```
argentina 3 brazil 0.
brazil's crest left the chat. 📉
```

## Rules
- celebrate the winner, never mock the losing nation's people — the joke is on
  the scoreboard and the pixels, not the country.
- keep it about the football.
