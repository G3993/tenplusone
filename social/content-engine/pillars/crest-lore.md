# Crest Lore

Pure love-of-the-game. One nation's pixel crest + a genuinely nerdy, true
history beat. This is the pillar that earns us the right to joke elsewhere.

## Visual
- The crest at hero scale on the grid, optional Higgsfield cinematic reveal
  (crest assembling from pixels over a stadium/texture plate).
- IG carousel: crest → 2-3 fact cards.
- YouTube: 30-60s Short, or longer mini-doc.

## Caption formula
`[nation] wear [visual detail]. here's why:`
`[1-2 true facts, told like a fan, not wikipedia].`

## Example
```
uruguay wear four stars. most people think two are wrong.
they're not. fifa counts the 1924 + 1928 olympic golds as
world titles, because back then the olympics WAS the world
championship. four stars. earned. argue with the suits, not us.
```

## Rules
- facts must be real and checkable. we are credible first.
- celebrate the nation. this pillar never trolls.
