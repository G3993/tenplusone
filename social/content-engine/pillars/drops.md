# Drops

The revenue pillar. Merch and crest releases tied to the shop
(internetfc.com). Always framed as "by the people, for the people," never as a
corporate ad.

## Visual
- Product on the crest grid (use the print masters), or a Higgsfield lifestyle
  shot of the garment with the pixel crest.
- Carousel: product → crest detail → grid system explainer.

## Caption formula
`[item]. every crest on the same 45×45 grid.`
`printed by us, for us. [link].`

## Example
```
the [nation] terminal tee just dropped.
every crest, one 45×45 grid. one constraint, endless variation.
printed on demand, shipped from the country closest to you.
your kit, your network. 🌐 internetfc.com
```

## Rules
- never the "thrilled to announce" voice. it's a drop, not a press release.
- tie the product back to the system (the grid, the constraint) — that's the
  story only we can tell.
- soft, not spammy: drops are ~10-15% of total volume, never the whole feed.
