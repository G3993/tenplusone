# The People's Desk

The troll pillar. Where we take on FIFA, corrupt federations, and the corporate
machine. This is the smallest pillar by volume and the highest by impact. It is
ALWAYS human-approved before posting. No exceptions.

## The enemy (read VOICE.md §3 in full)
- punch UP: FIFA corruption, sportswashing, ticket gouging, sponsor overreach,
  the suits selling our game.
- never DOWN: players, fans, nations, cultures, tragedies.

## Tone
Righteous + witty, not bitter. The underdog with a point. A good Desk post
makes fans nod AND laugh. Both, or it doesn't ship.

## Caption formula
`[real fifa / corporate fact today].`
`[dry one-line reframe].`
`owned by the people, not by them. 🌐`

## Example
```
fifa made ~$11bn off the last cup.
ticket prices for this one are up to $6,730.
the game is priceless. they just keep putting a price on it.
owned by the people, not by them. 🌐
```

## Hard rules
- must be tied to a REAL, verifiable event/fact. no invented outrage.
- never name-and-shame an individual player or fan.
- if it reads as mean instead of righteous, kill it.
- a human approves every single Desk post. this folder's drafts never
  auto-post even after we loosen the gate elsewhere.
