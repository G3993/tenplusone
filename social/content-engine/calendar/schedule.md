# iFC Posting Calendar

The rhythm. Generated from the WC2026 fixture list (`matches.ts`) + milestone
triggers. Tournament window: June 11 – July 19, 2026.

## Weekly baseline (pre-tournament)
| Day | Pillar | Platforms |
|---|---|---|
| Mon | crest lore | IG, YT |
| Tue | countdown / hype | IG, TikTok, X |
| Wed | the people's desk (gated) | X, TikTok |
| Thu | crest lore | IG, FB |
| Fri | drop / merch | IG, TikTok |
| Sat | countdown / community | all |
| Sun | crest lore + bracket talk | IG, X |

## During the tournament (per fixture)
- **T-18h** matchday card (auto-draft)
- **T-1h** "kickoff in 60" story/reply
- **FT** Battle of the Pixels (result-driven)
- **nightly** day recap stitch (YT Short + IG carousel)

## Milestone triggers (countdown pillar)
100 / 50 / 30 / 14 / 7 / 3 / 1 days to kickoff, group draw, fixtures release,
bracket lock, R32/R16/QF/SF/Final start, champion crowned.

## Volume guardrail
~1-2 posts/day pre-tournament, ramping to 3-5/day during. Drops stay ≤15% of
total. The People's Desk stays ≤2/week and always human-approved.
